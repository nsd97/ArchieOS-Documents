## Follow up Boss

# Complete List of Follow Up Boss Features

Follow Up Boss is a lead-focused real estate CRM designed for high-volume teams and brokerages. It emphasizes fast lead processing, team accountability, and seamless communication workflows. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of every feature available in the platform.

---

## Core CRM Features

### Lead Management & Organization

- **Lead Import/Export** — Import leads from 200+ sources (Zillow, Trulia, Realtor.com, Facebook, email, etc.); export data anytime[1](about:blank#fn1)[2](about:blank#fn2)
- **Lead Capture** — Automatic lead creation from websites, emails, and third-party sources[3](about:blank#fn3)
- **Smart Lists** — Unlimited custom lead lists with filtering by stage, source, activity, tags, and properties viewed[4](about:blank#fn4)
- **Lead Organization with Stages** — Customizable pipeline stages for tracking lead progression[5](about:blank#fn5)
- **Lead Tagging & Segmentation** — Pre-built tags and unlimited custom tags for organizing leads[6](about:blank#fn6)
- **Lead Source Tracking** — Track and attribute leads to specific sources with conversion metrics[7](about:blank#fn7)[8](about:blank#fn8)
- **Contact Management** — Store and retrieve complete contact information with activity history[9](about:blank#fn9)
- **Collaborators on Leads** — Multiple team members can collaborate on a single lead[10](about:blank#fn10)
- **Notes & Comments** — Full activity timeline with notes, mentions, and conversation history[11](about:blank#fn11)

### Lead Scoring & Prioritization

- **Lead Scoring Analysis** — Identifies hot, warm, and cold leads based on behavior[12](about:blank#fn12)
- **Predictive Lead Prioritization (AI-Powered)** — AI surfaces highest-value leads to contact first based on engagement patterns[13](about:blank#fn13)
- **Contact Tracking** — Logs all contact attempts, calls, texts, and emails per lead[14](about:blank#fn14)
