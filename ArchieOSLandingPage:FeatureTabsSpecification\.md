Persistent above all tabs. The single voice memo that triggers everything below.

**Waveform Visualization**

- Horizontal audio waveform graphic
- Duration indicator: `0:34`

```
"Just hung up with Sarah Chen she wants to list 42 Maple probably 1.2 maybe 1.3 detached four bed three bath she's flexible but wants photos this week oh and she's got a tenant until the 15th..."
```

## Responsive Considerations

### Desktop (1024px+)

- Full two-column layouts as specified

### Tablet (768px - 1023px)

- Maintain two-column where possible
- Reduce photo sizes
- Stack columns if needed

### Mobile (< 768px)

- Stack columns vertically (left above right)
- Tab navigation: Horizontal scroll
- Input block: Full width, smaller waveform
- Browser mockup: Scale down, maintain aspect ratio
